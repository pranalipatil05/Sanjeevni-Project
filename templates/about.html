What is Sanjeevani?

Sanjeevani is a real-time emergency healthcare platform designed to reduce critical delays during medical emergencies. It enables citizens, hospitals, and blood banks to coordinate instantly during life-threatening situations.

The platform focuses on one core problem: lack of reliable, real-time availability of hospital beds and blood units.

Why Sanjeevani Matters

⏱ Time-Critical

In emergencies, minutes matter. Sanjeevani eliminates guesswork and phone calls by showing live availability.

Hospital-Verified

Data is updated directly by hospitals and blood banks to ensure accuracy and trust.

Blood Access

Immediate visibility of blood group availability can save lives in trauma and surgical emergencies.

How It Works

Users search by city or emergency requirement. The system fetches live data from registered hospitals and blood banks using secure APIs.

Hospitals update bed and blood inventory through a dedicated dashboard, ensuring real-time synchronization.

Vision

Sanjeevani aims to become a nationwide emergency response backbone, integrating hospitals, blood banks, ambulances, and public emergency services into a single intelligent system.
